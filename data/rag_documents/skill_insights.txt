Skill Demand Intelligence Report 2024

AI/ML skills dominate the 2024 growth rankings with unprecedented velocity.

Prompt Engineering leads all skills at 149.03% YoY growth — the fastest 
skill adoption ever recorded in this platform. Organizations are hiring 
prompt engineers at every level from junior to principal.

Large Language Models (136.27%), Generative AI (121.45%), and OpenAI API 
(115.62%) follow, forming the core AI stack employers are hiring for in 2024.

Vector Databases at 97.29% growth signals that RAG-based applications 
are entering mainstream enterprise adoption.

LangChain (82.61%) and Hugging Face Transformers (66.45%) round out the 
AI framework layer — both are now considered foundational skills for 
AI engineering roles.

Traditional skills still in strong demand: Python, SQL, Cloud Computing, 
and Data Analysis maintain high absolute demand scores despite lower 
growth rates, as they form the foundation beneath AI skills.

Skill combination with highest salary premium in 2024:
Python + SQL + Machine Learning + Cloud = Senior ML Engineer profile
commanding $96,855 average salary.

Global Job Market Intelligence Report 2024

The global job market in 2024 is characterized by three dominant forces: 
AI skill premiumization, remote work normalization, and automation-driven 
role displacement.

Hiring volumes reached 795,002 tracked postings globally in 2024, 
representing 5.3% YoY growth. The technology sector leads hiring demand, 
followed by healthcare and financial services.

Remote work has stabilized at 46.2% of all postings, indicating permanent 
structural change in talent markets rather than a temporary trend.

The salary landscape shows 9.3% YoY growth to a global average of $63,012 USD. 
This growth significantly outpaces inflation, driven primarily by AI and 
engineering role premiums.

Top hiring geographies: Switzerland, United States, Norway, Singapore, 
and Germany dominate in both volume and salary levels.
